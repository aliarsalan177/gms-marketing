Verified biometric hardware. Nothing to guess about.

GMS has been tested end-to-end against every device below — from live scan to door open to attendance log to auto-suspend to re-enable on renewal.

✅ Recommended

  • ZKTeco K20 — 500 fingerprints, small gyms
  • ZKTeco K30 — 1000 fingerprints, most gyms
  • ZKTeco K50 / K90 — 3000+ fingerprints, large gyms
  • ZKTeco SpeedFace-V5L — face + fingerprint, contactless

👍 Also supported

  • ZKTeco MA300 — outdoor, IP65 rated
  • ZKTeco iClock260 — enterprise / chains
  • ZKTeco MB160 — face + fingerprint combo

⚠️ Avoid

  • ZKTeco K40 — firmware bug wedges TCP sessions. Use K30 instead.

🔧 What "supported" means

Persistent connection via the bundled Windows bridge service, real-time scan push, offline catchup, remote enrolment, auto user sync, per-member suspend/reactivate. No manual protocol tweaking.

From new member to check-in in under 90 seconds.

  1. 1

    Register the member

    Name, phone, photo. Optional CNIC and emergency contact. Auto-generated member code.

  2. 2

    Sell them a package

    Pick from your catalog or build a custom package. Full or partial payment, cash or transfer, receipt printed instantly.

  3. 3

    Enrol their fingerprint

    Click Capture on their page → they place a finger on the scanner → done. GMS auto-reconnects the device if it's stale.

  4. 4

    They scan on their way in

    Scanner recognises them → door opens → attendance logged → reception gets a toast showing their name + package status.

  5. 5

    Their package expires

    GMS auto-suspends their access. Scanner denies them until they renew. Renewal auto-reactivates. Zero manual bookkeeping.
